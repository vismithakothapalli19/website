What is AlexaVerse?

AlexaVerse is a forward-thinking event where developers, designers, and thinkers gather to explore the intersection of voice, AI, and creativity.

  • Keynote sessions with industry leaders
  • Hands-on workshops and live coding demos
  • Networking and collaboration opportunities

  • API and SDK showcases for conversational interfaces
  • Hackathon-style rapid prototyping lounge
